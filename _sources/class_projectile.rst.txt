Projectile
==========

**Inherits:** :ref:`RigidBody2D<class_RigidBody2D>` **<** :ref:`PhysicsBody2D<class_PhysicsBody2D>` **<** :ref:`CollisionObject2D<class_CollisionObject2D>` **<** :ref:`Node2D<class_Node2D>` **<** :ref:`CanvasItem<class_CanvasItem>` **<** :ref:`Node<class_Node>` **<** :ref:`Object<class_Object>`

A shootable element that causes damage to :ref:`Entity<class_Entity>` on impact and self-destucts. Can be shot using a :ref:`ProjectileAttack<class_ProjectileAttack>`.

If it hits anything other than an :ref:`Entity<class_Entity>`, it will self-destruct without applying damage.
